Eduardo Zanetti is a
south american artist,
self-taught and multi-
instrumentist.
He creates
music
and also
re-replays
classics of all
styles and eras,
adding audio layers
with each musical instrument
and thus creating a sound
context.
With
its roots
in classical rock,
it moves between
the genres of rock, jazz,
lounge, hip hop and deep house.
Having fun in the process,
aim to create fun but
also deep music.
Always looking
into generate
connection
between
genres
and
generations.
